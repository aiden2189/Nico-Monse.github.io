Common Questions

FAQs

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance. Since we both work full-time alongside our creative work, we need time to plan around schedules and request time off for shoots. For larger events like weddings, the more notice the better. Our availability is flexible — weekends, weekdays, whatever works for the project — but advance booking is key.

What's included in the turnaround time?

+

Turnaround depends on the size of the project. Quick shoots like drone clips, real estate tours, and small photo sets are typically delivered within about a week. Medium projects with longer videos or bigger photo sessions take around 2–3 weeks. Larger projects like weddings and full-day events with photo + video packages take 3–4 weeks. All timelines include full editing, color grading, and retouching.

Do you travel outside the 50-mile radius?

+

Absolutely. For projects outside our standard service area, we add a travel fee based on distance. We've shot across Wisconsin and are open to traveling further for the right project.

Can I get both photo and drone/video in one session?

+

Yes — that's one of the things that makes us unique. Monse covers photography while Nico handles all drone and video work, so you get a full visual package in a single session. Our Standard and Premium packages are designed for exactly this.

What if the weather is bad on shoot day?

+

If conditions aren't safe for flying or ideal for the shoot, we'll reschedule at no extra cost. We monitor forecasts closely and will communicate ahead of time if we need to adjust.

Are your drone pilots FAA certified?

+

Yes. Nico holds an FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate. All drone operations comply with federal regulations. Safety and legality are never compromised.

Do you offer raw files?

+

We typically deliver fully edited files. Raw files can be provided upon request for an additional fee, as our editing and color grading are a core part of the final product.
